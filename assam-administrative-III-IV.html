Assam Administrative Staff College Recruitment 2022 | 13300 Vacancies | For Class III & IV | Job Details | Age Limit | Educational Qualification | Apply Link

Company Description:

Assam Administrative Staff College is an Institute For Civil Service Training which is operated by India's Govt. of Assam. It's location is in the Heart of Assam, Guwahati. Candidates who are citizens of India and have an employment exchange number of Assam state and eligible for this job can apply for this job within 30th May, 2022.

Name of the Examination:

Assam Administrative Staff College Analogous Posts Class III and IV Examination 2022.

Total Vacancies:

13300 Posts.

Salary:

As per the Assam Government policy.

Age Limit :

Details for Age limit for Assam Administrative Staff College Analogous Post Class III and IV:
  1. The Applicant shall not be less than 18 years or more than 40 years of age as 01-01-2022

  2. The AASC Analogous Post Class III & IV age limit is relaxable for the below candidates:
    • 5 years for the SC/ST candidates/h5>
    • 3 years for OBC /MOBC candidates
    • 2 years for Ex-Serviceman
    • 10 years for Persons with Disabilities Candidates
